AI in Other Fields

Aside from the two other sections, AI has had its fair share of impact in different AI fields as well. Among them are cybersecurity, cloud computing, IT operations etc.

Future expectations

In all these fields of IT mentioned, it is very high possibility that the definition of the fields themselves change as well.

Are humans needed anymore?

The answer depends on the question intended. Humans are no longer needed in basic stuff that the AI tools do in a matter of seconds. As for complicated tasks, AI can be said to be approaching that level, but not yet there currently.
