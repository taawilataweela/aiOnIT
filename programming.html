AI in Programming

Programming is being influenced by AI to a great extent. This is why there have been questions regarding the future of programming in light of the recent developments of the likes of ChatGPT, Microsoft Copilot, and other LLMs.

What difference does AI make?

The main difference AI makes in coding is speeding up the process to a great extent. Other than that, AI can also code entire web pages, backend projects, and the likes which enable even the most beginner-level coder to have a great reach at what he can do. This is also the case to a great extent in Software Development and Engineering.

Are humans needed anymore?

Currently, yes. Though AI is really good at coding, it still makes a lot of mistakes when the process gets more complicated. Where humans can solve the underlying problems, because of the way AI is programmed, it is harder for it to come up with a new way of solution to more unique problems.
